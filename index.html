Calorie Crafter

Calorie Crafter is a website designed to organize and monitor people's dietary habits. With Calorie Crafter, users can receive personalized daily menu recommendations based on their body metrics and goals. Additionally, they can log their meals throughout the day into our system and track their daily intake of calories, carbohydrates, protein, and fat both textually and visually.Calorie Crafter is not published project but you can access the source code of this project on Github.
